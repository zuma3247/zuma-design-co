Case Study: African Construction Middle East (ACME)

The Client

ACME, which stands for African Construction Middle East, is an ambitious construction company launching in Dubai's competitive market. Founded by entrepreneurs with deep roots in African craftsmanship, the company sought to establish itself as a modern, reliable construction partner while honoring its cultural heritage.

The Challenge

The client needed a brand identity that would stand out in Dubai's crowded construction industry. They wanted something that felt contemporary and professional enough for corporate clients, yet distinctive enough to reflect their unique African background. The challenge was finding the right balance between global appeal and cultural authenticity.

Our Approach

We developed a modern brand identity that weaves traditional African patterns into a clean, professional design language. The logo uses bold geometric shapes inspired by traditional African textile patterns, rendered in a contemporary style that feels at home in Dubai's modern business landscape.

  • Created a distinctive logo combining geometric precision with African pattern influences
  • Developed a color palette featuring earthy tones balanced with professional neutrals
  • Designed business cards, letterheads, and presentation templates
  • Built brand guidelines ensuring consistent application across all materials

The Results

The final brand identity positions ACME as a forward-thinking construction company with a memorable visual presence. The African-inspired patterns have become a signature element, helping the company stand out in client presentations and marketing materials. The client reported positive feedback from potential partners who appreciated the unique cultural touch combined with professional polish.

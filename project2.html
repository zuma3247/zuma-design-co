Case Study: innov8 Living & i8 Hotels

The Client

innov8 Living is a new co-living concept launching in Dubai, designed for young professionals and digital nomads seeking community-focused living experiences. Their sister brand, i8 Hotels, offers boutique hotel stays with the same modern, connected philosophy.

The Challenge

The client was entering Dubai's competitive hospitality market with a fresh concept: flexible, stylish living spaces that blur the line between long-term residence and hotel stay. They needed a cohesive brand identity that would appeal to millennials and Gen Z travelers while communicating quality and innovation. The challenge was creating print materials that matched their modern, tech-forward positioning.

Our Approach

We crafted a sleek, contemporary visual identity built around clean lines and a sophisticated color palette. The design system emphasizes minimalism and functionality, reflecting the streamlined living experience the spaces offer. Every touchpoint was designed to feel premium yet approachable.

  • Developed a unified brand identity connecting innov8 Living and i8 Hotels
  • Designed a premium booklet showcasing amenities and floor plans
  • Created business cards with a modern, memorable layout
  • Established typography and color standards for all marketing materials

The Results

The completed brand package gave innov8 Living and i8 Hotels a distinctive presence in Dubai's market. The booklet design has been praised by early investors and partners for its clean presentation and effective communication of the living concept. The business cards have become conversation starters at networking events, helping the team make memorable first impressions.
